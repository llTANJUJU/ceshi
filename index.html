About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping survival action game where you battle against hordes of monsters in an ever-changing world. With roguelike elements and a variety of characters to choose from, every playthrough offers a unique experience.

As you progress, unlock powerful weapons, abilities, and upgrades to enhance your chances of survival. How long can you last against the relentless monster waves?

Game Features

Action-Packed Gameplay

Fast-paced combat with intuitive controls that are easy to learn but challenging to master.

Multiple Characters

Choose from a variety of unique characters, each with their own special abilities and play styles.

Power Upgrades

Collect power-ups and level up your character with a wide range of skills and weapons.

Endless Challenge

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters and see how long you can survive.

Roguelike Elements

Procedurally generated levels ensure no two playthroughs are ever the same.

Free to Play

Enjoy Monster Survivors completely free, right in your browser without any downloads.

How to Play

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.

Level Up

Collect experience orbs from defeated monsters to level up. Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones each time you level.

Survive

The longer you survive, the stronger the monsters become. Collect power-ups, manage your health, and utilize your abilities strategically to overcome the challenges.
